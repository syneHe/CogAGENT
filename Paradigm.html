Paradigm

Unified Knowledge Enhanced Paradigm

Knowledge Acquisition

Knowledge acquisition, the first step towards our knowledge-enhanced paradigm, aims at detecting knowledge concealed beneath the raw texts.

  • World Knowledge
  • Linguistic Knowledge
  • Commonsense Knowledge

Knowledge Representation

The aforementioned knowledge can be represented in two forms, including discrete representation and continuous representation.

  • Discrete Representation
  • Continuous Representation

Knowledge Injection

Injecting knowledge into original models is vital to the whole paradigm. The injection strategy varies depending on when knowledge is injected during the working process of the original language understanding model.

  • Knowledge-enriched Input
  • Knowledge-aware Architecture
  • Knowledge-assisted Training

Knowledge Application

Various downstream NLU tasks can benefit from the knowledge-enhanced models

  • Text Classification
  • Text Matching
  • Sequence Labeling
  • Machine Reading Comprehension
